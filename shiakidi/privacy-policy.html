PRIVACY POLICY

Shiakidi Beauty offers a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee on all orders. If your order does not exceed your highest expectations, we will refund the amount for all unused, unopened merchandise from your order addressed within 30 days from receipt of the order. Note, the shipping fee to an order is nonrefundable.

RETURN QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Order must be purchased from shiakidibeauty.com.
  • Products that are unused and unopened are eligible for a return.
  • No visible signs of use including lash glue or any discrepancy with the product.
  • Products must be in original packaging.
  • Gift Sets and Kits must be returned in full.
  • Final Sale Items cannot be returned.

RETURNS FOR CY CUSTOMERS

Shiakidi Beauty wants to make returns as simple as possible! Orders can be send to ACS courier Larnaca main shop. A 5€ Shipping and Handling fee is applied to all CY Orders.

Step 1: Complete your return request here. Please allow 2-4 business days (additional time may be needed during weekends and Holidays) for a Return Specialist to approve the return. If your return is approved, you will receive your return instructions via email. If your return is disapproved, you will receive an email confirming the reason.

Step 2: Pack the approved return item(s) in the original shipping package. Send the order to ACS courier Larnaca main shop or Akis Express Chrysopolitissa shop (Larnaca). Please ensure your order Invoice or receipt is included in the package.

Step 3: Your return will be processed within 10 business days of being delivered. Refunds are made in the original form of payment and are issued to the person documented on the original order as the "Bill To" Customer. If Store Credit is selected, an E-gift code will be sent to the email on file once the return is processed and ready for use on shiakidibeauty.com

DEFECTIVE OR DAMAGED ITEMS

If you receive any damaged or defective products, please address your concern to info@shiakidibeauty.com with the customer name, order number, and photo of the damaged or defective product(s) within 30 days from receipt date. Shiakidi Beauty can only accept orders placed on shiakidibeauty.com. If Shiakidi Beauty considers the items damaged or defective, the product will be replaced by shiakidibeauty.com. If the item is addressed after 30 days, the item is only eligible for store credit. All shipments for the replacement products will be subject to the same shipping service selected for the original order. We will not upgrade shipping service and can only upgrade the shipping service with additional payment from customer.

MISTAKE ORDERS

If your package is incomplete please send a photo of your entire order including the packing slip to info@shiakidibeauty.com within 10 days from delivery date. Once confirmed by Shiakidi Beauty the missing item(s) will replaced by shiakidibeauty.com. After the given timeframe Shiakidi Beauty is subject to deny the mistake inquiry.
