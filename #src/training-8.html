ABOUT THE COURSE

This is our most popular course because students gain the same amount of knowledge that is given in 2 separate seminars for beginner and advanced level training.

The first part of the course is aimed at studying the technology of eyelashes extension and modeling. The theory is divided into blocks, so each student has time to absorb all the information. A theoretical block is followed by practical exercises under the guidance of the trainer. Each type of lashes extensions will be practiced on a mannequin first and after on the real model. At the end of these 3 days, each student receives 2 prestigious certificates from us in English.

After this course, each student can apply for the possibility of an internship at Shiakidi Beauty studio. The most talented and hard-working students will be considered for employment at our studio or referenced to our friendly partners across the island.

*Coffee break and snacks are provided on all 3 days of the training.
