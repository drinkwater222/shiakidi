SHIAKIDI BEAUTY

SHIAKIDI BEAUTY is the industry’s leader with high quality, innovative Lash and Brow tools and supplies in Cyprus.
The SHIAKIDI BEAUTY is a private company and brand founded in 2017. The company specialises in eyelash extensions, lash lift & brow lamination trainings and supplies. The brand represents high-end luxury products for eyelash extensions, lash lift & brow lamination. As it is our main brand, we aim to make all products with consistency on its quality and designs, so clients can use the products with trust and satisfaction. All of the products undergo company’s strict quality control system.
SHIAKIDI ACADEMY - Our Eyelash Extensions and Lash lift & Brow lamination training courses are designed to provide the best possible learning experience to become a highly skilled Lash & Brow stylist. A specialized curriculum ensures our students get the best knowledge and hands on training to deliver their services with skill & confidence.
